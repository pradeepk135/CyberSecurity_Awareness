# Training Videos

This directory contains the professional corporate training videos for the Security Awareness Portal.

## Required Videos

Please add the following MP4 videos to this directory:

### Module 1: Why Security Matters
- **Filename**: `module-01-why-security-matters.mp4`
- **Content**: Professional corporate training video with Synersys branding (blue, orange, gray colors)
- **Style**: 2D flat cartoon style
- **Scene**: Confident office worker in light blue shirt with dark hair pointing directly forward at viewer

### Module 2: Company Policies  
- **Filename**: `module-02-company-policies.mp4`
- **Content**: Professional corporate training video with Synersys branding
- **Style**: 2D flat cartoon style
- **Scene**: Three animated icon cards appear sequentially. Card 1 shows fishing hook going through email envelope

### Module 3: Phishing and Social Engineering
- **Filename**: `module-03-phishing-social-engineering.mp4`
- **Content**: Professional corporate training video with Synersys branding
- **Style**: 2D flat cartoon style
- **Scene**: Office worker with short dark hair in light blue shirt sitting at modern desk working happily

### Module 4: Passwords and Account Security
- **Filename**: `module-04-passwords-account-security.mp4`
- **Content**: Professional corporate training video with Synersys branding
- **Style**: 2D flat cartoon style

## Video Specifications

- **Format**: MP4 (H.264)
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 (16:9 aspect ratio)
- **Branding**: Use Synersys colors:
  - Navy: #0f2138
  - Orange: #f26b1f  
  - Gray: #eef0f5
- **Style**: Professional 2D flat cartoon animation
- **Audio**: Clear voice narration with background music

## Integration

Once videos are added to this directory, they will automatically appear in the training modules. The system will:

1. Display the actual video instead of placeholder
2. Show chapter overlays with titles
3. Maintain progress tracking
4. Provide play/pause controls
5. Support seeking within videos

## Notes

- Videos should be optimized for web streaming
- Keep file sizes reasonable (under 100MB per video if possible)
- Ensure consistent branding and style across all videos
- Test videos in the application before deployment
